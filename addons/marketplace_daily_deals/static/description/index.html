Odoo Marketplace Daily Deals And Flash Sales

Attract your Customers with exciting Daily Offers in your Odoo Marketplace!

Odoo Marketplace Daily Deals And Flash Sales allows you and your sellers to publish daily discounts on your Odoo Marketplace. So, offer attractive discounts and attract more customers to your marketplace.

Daily Deals to Convert Window Shoppers into Buyers

Customers are always attracted towards the products on which they are getting discounts. Moreover, such discounts can also help the sellers to retain their Old customers. Such customers are already aware of the goods and services offered; hence, such exciting deals bring back their spark to shop again.

Many times, people tend to just scroll through online stores without intentions of actually buying; however, when they see the attractive discounts are going to end soon then they immediately buy the product.

As, your sellers will get more customers then other sellers would also like to team up with you; eventually, helps you to expand your business.

Engaging Customers to Your Online Store

When the customers get to know that there are some deals offered in your store on a daily basis then there are more chances for them to visit your store whenever they want to shop. Even, when they find the offers really reasonable then they can also refer your store to their friends and family.

So, let your sellers offer daily deals to your customers easily on your Odoo Marketplace. They can offer lucrative discounts on any product for particular time period.

Put Creative Banners to Seek Attention

Visual catches attention more than what we hear! People are attracted towards what they see more than that they hear so why not seek their attention with some attractive visuals.

Now, you can put a creative banner for the deals to catch the eyes of the store visitors along with some catchy description. Moreover, you can also blur or delete the expired offers as per your convenience.

The blurred offers will let your customers know the type of discounts you offer. Hence, such blurred deals will let the customers visit your site again to check the discounts.

Features

Offer Daily Deals to your Customers

  • Sellers can post their daily deals and discounts on your store.
  • Moreover, you can also post such flash sales on their behalf.
  • However, a seller can add only his/her product in the deal.
  • You can publish/unpublish any discount anytime.

Set the Products For Discount

  • You can add one or more products for discounts.
  • Moreover, you can also offer daily discounts for any particular variant.

Set the Offer Configuration Globally or Locally

  • You can set the offer configuration on global level,
  • Or, can override the global configuration for any particular deal/discount.

Separate Section for the Flash Sales on your Marketplace

  • The daily deals are shown on the 'Deals&Offers' section.
  • Hence, the customers can see all the offers from one place.

Put the Active Duration of the deal

  • Add the start date along with the end date of the deals and offers

View of Expired Deals on your Odoo Marketplace

  • You/Seller can set to delete the expired deals.
  • Or, blur them for the frontend view.

Convey Offer Expiry Messages

  • You/seller can show a message before/after the expiry of the offer
  • Also, set the time period before to show the message prior to offer expiry

Set the Display for the offers on Odoo Marketplace

  • You can set the grid view as well as sticker view
  • You can display banners only, products only or both for the offer

Set Offer Page Header to seek customer attention

  • You have a choice to show the page header for the Offer page.
  • Also, add the page header text as per your wish.

Set the pricelist to offer Discounts

  • You can set/change the pricelist for the the offers as you require

Customer can view all the Deals and Discounts from one place

Set the Global Configuration for Odoo Marketplace Daily Deals And Flash Sales

Set the Configuration for Each Discount/Deal

  • Add the description For the Discount
  • Add the banner to Show in the Daily Deal

Set the Configuration for Each Discount/Deal only for his/her Products

  • Add the description For the Discount
  • Add the banner to Show in the Daily Deal
