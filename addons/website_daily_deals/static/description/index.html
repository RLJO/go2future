Website Deals And Flash Sales

Website Deals and Flash Sales module allows you to create the Deals and Flash sales for your customers.

The discount on the deals depends on the price lists. If you have pricelist for product , then you need to add that product in the deal for showing in the website. You can customize the view of the deals in website by your own. By using this module you can easily promote and enhance your business.

Salient Features
  • Deal can be created in very simple steps.
  • Discount on the deals is applied through price lists.
  • Discount on the product will be displayed in the website automatically after adding the product in a particular deal.
  • Apart from the discount other offers can be applied on the products also.
  • Admin have full control on customizing the view from back end.
  • Admin can customize the view from front also.
  • Deals can be shown both in Grid view or in the Slider view.
  • Quick View for Each Product in the deal.
  • Countdown Clock/Timer for each and every Deal.
  • Customize the messages you want to show in website by your own.
  • Set the default Configuration for all deals or for a single Deal.
  • It has many more amazing features described below.

Product Prices And Discounts

Grid View of the products in daily deals page

  • The Deals Can be displayed in the grid view.Each deal product has a link its actual product.
Slider View of the products daily deals page.

  • The Products in the deal can be also displayed in the Slider view.
Deal Product Options

  • There is a Quick View and Chose Option button for each product, Options will be visible after hovering the product.
Quick View of each Product

Blurred Deals after expiry

  • When a deal is expired it will be blurred or it can be deleted from the website.Also the message after expiration will be displayed after expiration.
Countdown Clock/Timer for each Deal

  • This Countdown Timer/Clock will show the time left in the deal expiration.
Show/Hide the Deals page in website

  • You can hide or show the deals page from the website by simply checking or unchecking this option.
