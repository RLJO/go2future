Manage Customized Bundles to Sell the Combinations together

When you offer a combination of products together then it offers ease to the customers. Hence, when a customer wishes to buy a product in combination then adding such a lot to the cart is not just hectic but along with that it wastes a lot of time. If there is a bundle already created for the products then it can minimize the tedious task of customers.

Moreover, you can also add different products as well as their variants to the bundle and add the price for the whole bundle together. Many times it happens that customers even purchase the product which they were not planning to buy just because the product is in the combination.

Odoo Website Customized Bundle Product facilitates you to create multiple bundle products where you can add multiple products as well as their variants or multiple quantities of the same product. Hence, it causes ease to your customers which increases their chance to shop with you again.

Offer Lucrative Product Pack Price to attract the Customers

Odoo Website Customize Bundle Products allows you to set the price for the bundle product as per your wish. A small discount in the product pack can let your customers buy the whole combination together.

Hence, it shows how much your customers are saving when they are purchasing a bundle of products instead of buying them separately. Moreover, the customer has authority to select or deselect any product from the bundle as per their wish.

It helps you to improve their sales as they can sell multiple products together as customers can heed the benefit clearly. The module offers flexibility to manage the bundle completely as they want.

Let the Customers Customize their Bundle as They Desire

Odoo Website Customize Bundle Products offers flexibility to your customers as they can pick any product they want to buy from the bundle. Hence, it gives them a chance to customize the bundle as they want.

The customer can select or deselect any product from the combination. Apart from that, customers can set the quantity by adding the input or can increase/decrease it with the help of a button. It offers flexibility to the customers which builds up credibility among customers and gives them a reason to visit your Webs again for the ease of shopping.
