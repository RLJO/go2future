Customized Bundle Products to Sell the Combinations together

Offering a combination of products together can offer ease to the customers. When a customer wishes to purchase a product in combination then adding such a lot to the cart is really hectic as well as time wasting. If there is a bundle created for the products then it can minimize the tedious task of customers.

Furthermore, you can also add different products as well as their variants to the bundle and add the price for the whole bundle together. Many times it happens that customers even buy the product which they were not planning to buy because the product is in a combination.

Odoo Marketplace Customized Bundle Product allows you and your sellers to create multiple bundle products where you can add multiple products as well as their variants or multiple quantities of the same product. Hence, it causes ease to your customers which increases their chance to choose you again.

Attract Your Customers with Lucrative Product Pack Price

Odoo Marketplace Product Pack allows you to set the price for the bundle product as per your wish. A small discount in the product pack can let your customers buy the whole combination together.

Hence, it shows how much your customers are saving when they are purchasing a bundle of products instead of buying them separately. Moreover, the customer has authority to select or deselect any product from the bundle as per their wish.

It helps your sellers to boost their sales as they can sell multiple products together as customers can notice the benefit clearly. The module offers flexibility to your sellers to manage the bundle completely as they want.

Let the Customers Customize their Bundle as They Want

The module offers flexibility to your customers as they can pick any product they want to buy from the bundle. Hence, it gives them a chance to customize the bundle as they want.

The customer can select or deselect any product from the combination. Apart from that, customers can set the quantity by adding the input or can increase/decrease it with the help of a button. It offers flexibility to the customers which builds up credibility among customers and gives them a reason to visit your marketplace again for the ease of shopping.
